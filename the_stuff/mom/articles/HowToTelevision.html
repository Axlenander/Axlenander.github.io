How to Use the Living Room T.V.

The first important thing to look at is simply the remotes for the T.V.

Remotes

  1. Power button
  2. Input button(important)
  3. Volume Rocker(n' roll)
  1. On button(turns on both T.V. and satelite box)
  2. Off button(turns off both T.V. and satelite box)

Note: the off button signal for the T.V. is the same as the on one, so if you turn only the T.V. off, and then want to turn off the cable box, when you press the button again, you'll trun the T.V. back on

  • the red button is power
  • the 1-5 buttons switch between the things connected to the switch(things noted later in article)

Note: Remote isn't the best, might need to click a couple times or change position to get action you preform to actually work

How to acess things

Satelite T.V.

  1. Take the Satelite Remote(the white one) and turn on the T.V. and Satelite Box(try to do it at the same time)
  2. Take T.V. Remote (the black one) and click the input button until the cursor is hovering over the satelite icon
  3. Should be working, if not, check for blue light on cable box, if there is no light, try to turn it on(it is off right now)
  4. if none of this works, contact me

Wii U(just dance)

  1. Turn on T.V. with Black (or White) Remote
  2. Turn on Wii U by pressing the power button on the front of the system(if blue light, it's turned on)
  3. grab the Black remote and press input until HDMI1 is selected
  4. Grab the switch remote(the small gray one) and turn on the switch by hitting the power button (a blue light should appear if it worked)
  5. switch the port to port 3 by pressing the number 3 on the remote
  6. you should be greeted by a screen with a couple profiles, and white in the background. Further setup is in another article(read here not yet implemented... as article has yet to exist)

ChromeCast

  1. As to be expected with most of these things, turn on the T.V. First with the Black (or White) Remote
  2. grab the Black Remote(T.V. remote) and press input to change the T.V. to HDMI2
  3. Connect phone or computer(for the moment, I'll just show you how to play a youtube video)
    1. Connect to the home wifi(yes you do have to do this)
    2. Open youtube on your phone, then the video you want to play
    3. Then, click the button that's a square in the upper right corner
    4. Then click "Family Room Tv"
    5. To discconect, simply click that same square button again and press the text at the bottom that says "stop casting"
